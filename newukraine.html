New Ukrainian School
In 2017, I started working together with the founders of the New Ukrainian School on integrated textbooks for pupils (1-2 classes). New Ukrainian School is the reform of the secondary school of the Ministry of Education and Science. In particular, I made all illustrations and layout of textbooks, as well as I participated in creating of creative tasks in these textbooks. I made 2 textbooks, and this is more than 200 illustrated pages.
